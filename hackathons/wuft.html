What is WUFT Hacks?

The Wharton Undergraduate FinTech (WUFT) Hackathon is a programming competition sponsored by private companies. The event brings together some of the brightest minds to solve challenges at the crossroads of business and technology.

What is the objective?

Competing as a team, you will face prompts related to digitizing personal financial well-being and credit. You will have 2 weeks to create a technological solution, present it in front of a panel of industry experts at our one day summit, and have the potential to win over $5,000.

What should I do after I register?

After you register, you will either be matched with other students or compete with your predetermined team. You can start coding as soon as the cases are distributed about 2 weeks before the event. Otherwise, begin brainstorming and reading about FinTech trends now!
